OUR SERVICES

- Ghana-weaving We make ghana-weaving of different styles ranging from shuku, all-back(cornrows), two-step, and all other styles.
- Braids We make different braid styles ranging from knotless braids, Goddess braids etc.
- Twists We also make different twists like micro-twists, maxi twists and kinky twists.
- Virgin hairstyles We make hairstyles for virgin hairs like weaving, didi, natural twists and bob Marley.
- Hair treatments We also offer hair treatment services for your hair like applying organic products, relaxing, and washing.
- Hair dyeing We help dye your hair to your preferred colors
- Wigs We also offer wig services like revamping and making of wigs from the start to your descriptions.
- Training services We have available slots for accepting apprentices to train them on all hair services and treatment of customers. We also make sure they gain all necessary knowledge before graduating.
